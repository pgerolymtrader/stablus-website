Welcome to Stablus

Where Corfu slows down to its most beautiful pace

Nestled in the tranquil village of Pagoi on the north-western coast of Corfu, Stablus is a lovingly maintained guest cottage that blends authentic Greek character with thoughtful modern comforts.

Wake to the sound of olive trees rustling in the Ionian breeze. Spend your days exploring hidden coves, Byzantine trails, and centuries-old villages — then return to the cool, whitewashed serenity of your own private retreat.

Whether you are seeking a romantic escape, a family holiday, or simply a few days of genuine rest, Stablus offers the space and stillness to find it.

Explore Corfu

Pagoi is your gateway to the island's best

Stablus sits in the unspoiled north-west of Corfu, close to some of the island's most celebrated beaches, villages, and natural wonders.

Beach

Sidari & Canal d'Amour

Famous for its unique sandstone formations and warm, turquoise waters. Legend has it couples who swim through the canal are bound together forever.

↠ 12 km from Stablus

Culture

Corfu Old Town

A UNESCO World Heritage Site of Venetian, French and British architecture, with winding alleyways, vibrant markets, and superb waterfront tavernas.

↠ 35 km from Stablus

Nature

Corfu's Ancient Olive Groves

With over four million olive trees, Corfu's interior is a lush paradise perfect for walking, cycling, and connecting with the island's deep agricultural heritage.

↠ On your doorstep

Beach

Logas Sunset Beach

Perched on dramatic cliffs above the sea, Logas (Peroulades) offers one of Greece's most photographed sunsets. A short and magical drive away.

↠ 8 km from Stablus

Dining

Local Tavernas & Cuisine

Discover authentic Corfiot flavours — sofrito, bourdeto, fresh seafood — in village tavernas where recipes have been passed down for generations.

↠ Within the village

Beach

Agios Stefanos

A peaceful, family-friendly fishing village beach just minutes away. Crystal-clear shallows, excellent snorkelling, and a handful of charming beach tavernas.

↠ 6 km from Stablus
